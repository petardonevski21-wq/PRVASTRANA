Skopje, Macedonia

After an incredible chapter in Soho, Sunday 17th May will be our final day trading at 362 West Broadway.

Skopje Macedonia

362 West Broadway was our first home in the US and has played a huge part in the growth of Thom Sweeney stateside. From first suits to full wardrobes, we are deeply grateful to everyone who has walked through our doors over the years.

From Wednesday 20th May, we will be operating from a temporary pop-up space at 30 Bond Street, where we will continue to offer Bespoke and Made to Measure appointments alongside our Spring Summer ready-to-wear collection.

During this transition, our team is also available for home and office visits. To book an appointment or find out more, please get in touch.

Our next New York project is just around the corner. One of the biggest moments in the Thom Sweeney story yet. Watch this space.
